What are your rates?

Typical rates are 200$ a day. One day typically involves 8 hours of work. Rates can be negotiable. If I want to work with your band I can work with any budget within reason.
Is recording to tape extra?
Yes. Tape is not free. If you decide to use tape on your project you can expect to use one reel a day at a rate of 30$ per reel
How do you decide what projects to work on?
Friends come first, then friends of friends. Whatever time I have left is dedicated to artists I feel I will vibe with.
Can you master my recording?
I believe a different set of ears than the engineer's and mixer's should be used. However, I'm happy to recommend world class mastering studios.
Can you produce my recording?
No. I am strictly a recording/ mix engineer. I do not believe in changing an artist's sound to fit my own preconceptions
